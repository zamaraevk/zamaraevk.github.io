ABOUT

I graduated as an Engineer from Ufa State Petroleum Technological University in Russia in 2009, where I studied the Computer Science fundamentals and first began building websites.

In 2014, I moved to New York and gained an in-depth perspective about modern web frameworks. Since then, I've worked for Creative Circle Agency as a freelancer and as an email marketing specialist at Affinity Solutions, where I used HTML and CSS to deliver sleek email marketing campaigns to millions of users.

I became increasingly interested in JavaScript and decided to spend my summer at MakerSquare — an intense software engineering training program. Here, I led agile teams in full-stack web and mobile app development, leveraging multiple frameworks including React, React Native, AngularJS, Node.js, MongoDB, and SQL.

I am currently seeking work on a fast-paced team where I can contribute my skill and passion for intuitive and responsive front-end interfaces combined with strategically architected backends. I look forward to hearing from you.
